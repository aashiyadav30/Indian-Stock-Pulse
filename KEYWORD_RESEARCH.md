# Keyword Research - Indian Stock Market

## Objective
Research and select high-value keywords for programmatic SEO targeting Indian stock market investors.

## Tools Used
- Google Keyword Planner
- Ubersuggest
- Google Trends
- Competitor analysis (MoneyControl, NSE India)

## Research Process

### 1. Primary Keywords Identified
| Keyword | Monthly Volume | Difficulty | Intent |
|---------|---------------|------------|---------|
| "indian stock prices" | 18,000 | Medium | Informational |
| "NSE live stock market" | 12,000 | High | Transactional |
| "BSE share prices today" | 8,000 | Medium | Informational |
| "[Company] share price" | Varies | Low-Med | Transactional |

### 2. Long-Tail Keywords (Lower Competition)
- "Reliance Industries share price today live NSE"
- "TCS stock price analysis 2026"
- "HDFC Bank share price 52 week high low"
- "IT sector stocks India list"
- "Banking stocks NSE BSE"

### 3. Keyword Selection Criteria
✅ Search volume: 1,000+ monthly searches
✅ Relevance: Direct match to our content
✅ Competition: Medium or lower
✅ Intent: Informational + transactional mix

### 4. Implementation in Pages

#### Homepage (/)
- **Target**: "Indian stock market", "NSE BSE stocks"
- **Title**: "Indian Stock Insights – Real-Time NSE & BSE Market Data"
- **H1**: "Indian Stock Insights"

#### Stock Detail Pages (/stocks/[symbol])
- **Target**: "[Company name] share price", "live stock price"
- **Title Pattern**: "{Company} Share Price Today, Live NSE Stock Price & Analysis"
- **Example**: "Reliance Share Price Today, Live NSE Stock Price & Analysis"

#### Sector Pages (/sectors/[sector])
- **Target**: "[Sector] stocks India", "best [sector] companies"
- **Title Pattern**: "{Sector} Stocks – Top {Sector} Companies in India"
- **Example**: "IT Stocks – Top Information Technology Companies in India"

### 5. Keyword Density & Placement
- **Title tags**: Primary keyword in first 60 characters ✅
- **Meta descriptions**: Include 1-2 keywords naturally ✅
- **H1 tags**: Main keyword ✅
- **Content**: Natural keyword integration (2-3% density) ✅
- **URLs**: Keyword-friendly slugs (/stocks/reliance) ✅

### 6. Competitive Analysis
**MoneyControl**: High domain authority, targets same keywords
**NSE India**: Official source, strong for brand keywords
**Our Strategy**: Focus on long-tail, user-friendly content

### 7. Expected Results
- **Organic traffic**: 10,000+ monthly visitors (6-12 months)
- **Rankings**: Top 10 for long-tail keywords (3-6 months)
- **Conversion**: High intent traffic from specific stock queries

## Conclusion
Selected keywords balance search volume, competition, and relevance to maximize SEO performance for Indian stock market content.
